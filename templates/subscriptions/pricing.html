Frequently asked questions

Do I need a credit card to sign up?▾
No. The free plan includes 10 scans with zero payment info required. Upgrade only when you need unlimited.
Which payment methods are accepted?▾
UPI (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm, or any UPI app) for Indian payments — scan the QR and tap "I've Paid". Or pay via PayPal for international payments.
Can I cancel anytime?▾
Yes. Cancel from your dashboard at any time. You keep access until the end of the current billing period.
Is my code stored?▾
Only to generate and display your scan history. We never sell it, share it with third parties, or use it to train AI models.
Is the yearly plan auto-renewed?▾
Yes, it auto-renews annually. You can cancel before the renewal date from your dashboard with no penalty.
